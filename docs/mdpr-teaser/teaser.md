## Context slimming is benchmarked with sufficiency gates

The benchmark reports 69.3% weighted saving and 63.5% median saving only for cases where the full context and reduced context both remain sufficient. Source: docs/benchmark.md.

| Signal | Value |
|---|---|
| Weighted saving | 69.3%; source: docs/benchmark.md |
| Median saving | 63.5%; source: docs/benchmark.md |
| Eligible success | 100%; source: docs/benchmark.md |
| Best observed saving | 88.3%; source: docs/benchmark.md |
